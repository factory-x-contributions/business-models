The documentation for business model development in Factory-X is now online. At its core is the **House Building Logic** – a proven methodology for data-driven business models in an industrial context.

{/* truncate */}

## The House Building Logic: From Entrance to Exit

The methodology is structured into six areas that guide teams step by step from the initial idea to a decision-ready proposal – with concrete tools instead of empty canvas fields.

- 🚪 **Entrance Area:** Define the business idea, conduct market analysis and set strategic direction
- 🔵 **Blue Room:** Understand customer needs – develop personas, pains, and gains
- 🔴 **Red Room:** Develop the value proposition – translate customer problems into concrete solutions
- 🟢 **Green Room:** Design value creation – plan partners, data flows, and operational implementation
- 🟡 **Yellow Room:** Validate economic viability – calculate costs, revenues, and pricing models
- 🚪 **Exit Area:** Bundle results, assess risks, and create a decision proposal

## What the Website Offers

In addition to the full methodology documentation, the website includes an **AI Assistant** section with prompts and skill cards for each step of the House Building Logic, as well as a **workshop format** for five-day intensive sessions with teams.
