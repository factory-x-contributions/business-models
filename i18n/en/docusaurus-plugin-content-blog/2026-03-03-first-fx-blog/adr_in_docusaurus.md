The documentation for business model development in Factory-X is now online. At its core is the **House Building Logic** – a proven methodology for data-driven business models in an industrial context.

<!-- truncate -->

## The Four Rooms of House Building Logic

- 🔵 **Blue Room:** Understanding customer needs
- 🔴 **Red Room:** Defining the value proposition
- 🟢 **Green Room:** Designing value creation
- 🟡 **Yellow Room:** Validating economic viability

The methodology guides teams step by step from the initial idea to a decision-ready proposal – with concrete tools instead of empty canvas fields.
